Server Rules

Follow these RP rules strictly to keep the server chill and fair for everyone 🔥

🚫 RDM (Random Deathmatch)

Killing someone without valid RP reason is not allowed. RP motive is must.

🚗 VDM (Vehicle Deathmatch)

Don't run over people without proper RP. Car = not a weapon!

🧠 Metagaming

Using OOC info in IC situations is not allowed. Keep it in RP world.

💪 Powergaming

Doing unrealistic or forced actions without giving chance to others is PG. Avoid it!

🔁 NLR (New Life Rule)

After death, forget everything from that scenario. No memory transfer!

😈 Revenge RP

No revenge immediately after death. Wait at least 30 mins before re-engaging.

🛑 Combat Logging

Logging out during RP/fight is strictly prohibited. Staff will notice!

🎯 Cop Baiting

Don’t provoke cops just to start chase/RP. Let RP happen naturally.

☠️ Spawn Killing

Killing someone as soon as they spawn = big NO. Give chance to RP.

😱 Fear RP

If someone has a weapon on you, fear for your life and act accordingly.

🚔 Cop Presence

Major crimes must include police presence or be notified to them.

🔥 Flow RP

Don’t break immersion or stop RP mid-way. Keep it smooth and realistic.

💥 Suppress Fire

Use warning shots before direct attack if possible. Don’t shoot first always.

✅ Proper RP

Use correct /me /do /try and give fair responses. No shortcut RP!

🕊️ Safe Zones

Green zones like hospital, spawn, and bank are non-RP zones. No fights here!

🚫 CDM (Car Deathmatch)

Don’t use vehicle to kill people unnecessarily. It’s PG + CDM.

👮 Admin Rules

Admins must stay unbiased and handle reports fairly. No admin abuse.

💼 Robbery RP

Robbery needs valid RP, planning, and time gap. No chain robbing!
